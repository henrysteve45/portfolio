Cybersecurity

I understand that securing digital information is critical to the operation of any organization, as we live in a time where many business and government functions are powered through cloud technology. Organizations must endure a never-ending battle of threat actors trying to penetrate their networks, while also defending against them with secure technology and best practices. My background and passion in cybersecurity will help ensure companies are resilient to these attacks.

Web Development

Web applications are what drive user engagement with products, ideas, and tools. Organizations need intuitive, modern and secure websites that demonstrate their mission and what impacts they have on the world. Creating the frontend of these sites includes eloquent HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code supported by modern design frameworks and libraries such as React, jQuery, and Bootstrap. My experience with these tools will ensure organizations and their users will have a great frontend to use.

Additionally, security must be included directly in the software development pipeline, instead of being a seperate entity. My security experience will ensure sites are created in alignment with security best practices in mind, including:

  • Compliance with international standards from organizations such as NIST, IEEE, and OWASP.
  • Secure coding techniques such as input validation, proper error handling, and verifying security of third-party libraries and SDKs.
  • Modern software development methodologies such as DevSecOps are used during development.
